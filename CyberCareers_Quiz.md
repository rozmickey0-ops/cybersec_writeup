### Task 1: Unfilled Positions
**Question:**  
How many unfilled cyber positions are there?

**Answer:**  
**3.5 million**

**Explanation:**  
The text states cybersecurity has over 3.5 million unfilled roles globally, showing high demand.

---

### Task 2: Security Analyst Role
**Question:**  
Security analysts play a significant role in an organisation’s _____?

**Answer:**  
**defence**

**Explanation:**  
Security analysts are called the digital defenders and monitor, investigate, and respond to activity to protect the organisation’s defence.

---

### Task 3: Security Engineer Device
**Question:**  
What is the name for the type of device that a Security Engineer would be responsible for maintaining?

**Answer:**  
**Intrusion Detection System (IDS)**

**Explanation:**  
The text describes IDS as a security camera within an organisation’s digital environment that Security Engineers maintain.

---

### Task 4: Penetration Testing Process
**Question:**  
What process do penetration testers follow when testing an organisation for weaknesses?

**Answer:**  
**An engagement**

**Explanation:**  
Everything is done under an agreement between the company and the pentester. This process is known as an engagement.

---

### Key Takeaways
- Cybersecurity has 3 major paths: defensive, engineering, and offensive.
- **Security Analyst**: Blue team, monitors and investigates alerts.
- **Security Engineer**: Builds and maintains defensive systems like IDS.
- **Penetration Tester**: Red team, simulates attacks under a formal engagement.
